Frequently Asked Questions There's no harm in asking

Hosting is a server, a place on the internet for your website

It allows you to reserve space on someone else's server and put your work online. You can compare it to paying rent for a plot of land that someone rents for you.

Hosting cost is not included in our estimate

Purchasing hosting will be your responsibility, which we can help with. Once the site is uploaded to hosting, we will be able to make remote changes without needing your login details.

We offer small changes for free for one year after completing the service.

Large changes — for example a new unique section or a complete redesign — take significantly more time and would be charged. However, tasks like replacing text, images, or small structure tweaks will be free for a year.
