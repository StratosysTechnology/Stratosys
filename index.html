Frequently Asked Questions knowledge is power

Hosting is a server, a place on the internet for your website

It allows you to reserve space for your website on someone else's server and introduce our work to the web. It can be compared to paying rent for a plot of land that someone rents for you.

Hosting cost is not included in our pricing

Purchasing hosting will be your task, which we can help with. After uploading the site to hosting, we'll be able to later include any changes remotely from our own computer without needing your login credentials.

We offer free minor changes for one year after completing the service contract.

It would be different if the changes were major, such as a new unique section or complete change of existing ones. Then it takes significantly more time. However, all changes like text replacement, images, small structural changes - these will be free for a year.
